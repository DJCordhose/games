Administration

What is a Game Jam?

  • People with a common interest meet and hack on game ideas together
  • Community based and organized
  • You bring your own ideas, possibly your own technology
  • NO COMPETITION - NO PRICES - NO WORKING AT NIGHT

Schedule for Friday: Preparation

  • 18:30 - 19:30 Introduction to Game Programming with Phaser
  • 19:30 - 20:00 People introduce their game ideas, 5 minutes each max.
  • 20:00 - 21:00 Teams form and plans are made based on ideas introduced before
  • 21:00 Formal End of Event for Friday

Schedule for Saturday: Coding and presentation

  • 10:00 - 18:00: You hack in teams
  • 18:00 - 19:00: Presentation of results
  • 19:00: Formal End of Event
  • Potentially informal beer drinking during presentation and afterwards
